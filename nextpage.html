My Favorite Manga/Anime

My favorite manga/anime is Knights of Sidonia. It is a fictional story set over a thousand years later in deep space, where Earth was destroyed by aliens called Gauna. I read the manga back in middle school, but recently refound the anime. I watched it then decided to get the manga that I read a long time ago.

I'll include some screenshots of what the Gauna look like. They absorb organic matter to grow stronger and adapt to situations thrown at them.

Learn more about the Gauna on the KoS Wiki.

Other Favorites

My other favorite anime/manga are Neon Genesis Evangelion and Ghost in the Shell. These have complex stories, deep themes, and impactful characters. Neon Genesis Evangelion is hard to follow so you need to look up some things. Ghost in the Shell is a very good anime. I also make custom Magic: The Gathering cards around these anime/manga, and sometimes D&D characters based on them.
