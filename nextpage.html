My Favorite Manga/Anime

My favorite manga/anime is Knights of Sidonia. It is a fictional story set over a thousand years later in deep space, where Earth was destroyed by aliens called Gauna. I read the manga back in middle school, but recently refound the anime. I watched it then decided to get the manga that I read a long time ago.

Here are some screenshots of what the Gauna look like. They absorb organic matter to grow stronger and adapt to situations thrown at them.

Other Favorites

My other favorite anime/manga are Neon Genesis Evangelion and Ghost in the Shell. These have complex stories, deep themes, and impactful characters. Neon Genesis Evangelion is hard to follow so you really need to look up some things to understand what's even happening sometimes. Ghost in the Shell is a much watch Anime. The complete manga is really good, but the final book is very hard to follow. I actually make custom Magic: The Gathering cards around these anime/manga. I also make D&D characters based around them too. I mostly do this when I'm bored.
