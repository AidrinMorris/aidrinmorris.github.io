My favorite manga/anime is Knights of Sidonia. Knights of Sidonia is a fictional story set over a thousand years later in space. Earth was destroyed by an alien race called the Gauna. Ships called "humanity seed ships" escaped as the Gauna invaded. The two main seedships that are mentioned in the story are seedship Sidonia and Aposimz, Sidonia being where the manga and anime take place.

Here are some screenshots of what the Gauna look like. The Gauna absorb organic matter to grow stronger and adapt. If they absorb a human, they will take on its appearance.

Learn more about the Gauna on the KoS Wiki.
